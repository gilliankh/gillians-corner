John

2020
C/C++ Ascii art interactive story

Based on a southern US folktale called "When John Gets Here" or "The Haunted House." A man looking for a place to stay the night finds a spooky house abandoned but full of nice amenities. He is repeatedly woken by larger and larger black cats that ask him "Are you gonna be here when John gets here?"
