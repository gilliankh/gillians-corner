Shell Reflection

2024
shell sculpture approx. 47 x 37 x 20in (119 x 94 x 51cm); metal stand 45in (114cm) tall
virtual interactive experience paired with shell sculpture

A shell almost big enough to climb in, next to a program that allows you to walk around inside. The footsteps of the virtual character can be heard coming from inside the real, physical shell. Virtual shell made with Unity and Blender.

Not only a tribute to my pet garden snails and to my journey of coming out of my own “shell,” but also an exploration of inside vs outside, comfort vs discomfort, and real vs replica.
